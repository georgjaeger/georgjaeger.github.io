About

Dr. rer. nat.
Georg Jäger

After obtaining my PhD in physics, I am now Assistant Professor for Computational Systems Sciences at the Institute of System Sciences, Innovation and Sustainability Research, University of Graz. My main research interests are the simulation and modelling of complex systems in fields relevant to the challenges society currently faces.



EXPERTISE:
Theory: Systems Science | Mathematics | Physics | Networks | Early Warning Signals
Methods: Agent-based Modeling | Equation-based Modeling | Machine Learning | Cellular Automata
Application: Sustainability | Innovation | Traffic and Mobility | Labor Market
